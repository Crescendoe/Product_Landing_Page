Hand-crafted candles for your next adventure

Will-O-Wicks is a small candle business that takes pride in creating a story with our candles.

Candles are hand-poured with soy wax and are inspired by the many exciting adventures that YOU create.

  • 100% soy wax
  • Hand-poured
  • Phthalate-free
  • Lead-free

We hope you enjoy our candles as much as we enjoy making them!

Fragrances

Adventure

Adventure is a blend of sandalwood, cedar, and vanilla. Perfect for those who love the great outdoors.

Wanderlust

Wanderlust is a blend of lavender, rosemary, and mint. For those who love to travel.

Enchanted

Enchanted is a blend of jasmine, rose, and sandalwood. Great for anyone who wants a bit of whismy in their day.

Cozy

Cozy is a blend of vanilla, cinnamon, and clove. Perfect for those who love to snuggle up with a good book.
